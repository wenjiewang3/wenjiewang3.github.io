Biography

I am a Ph.D. student at the School of Mathematics and Statistics at the University of Melbourne. I received my B.E. degree in Communication Engineering from Huazhong University of Science and Technology and my M.S. degree from the same university in 2022. My research mainly focuses on kernel-based causal discovery and causal representation learning.
